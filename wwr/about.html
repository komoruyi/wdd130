Rapids Adventure Company

Here in kenneth Leisure Adventure Company, we believe that life's greatest adventures happen when you step outside your comfort zone. Our mission is to provide safe, thrilling, and unforgettable white water rafting experiences that connect people with nature's raw power and beauty. We are committed to environmental stewardship, safety excellence, and creating memories that last a lifetime. Come join us as we navigate the rushing waters and discover the adventurer within you!

History

Founded in 2010 by outdoor enthusiasts kenneth leisure, The company which is know as kenneth Leisure adventure Company began as a small family operation with just four rafts and a dream to give people the best fun of their lives with water rafting with others. What started as weekend trips for friends and family has grown into one of the world most trusted and reliable rafting company.

Over the past fifteen years, we've safely guided over 2000 adventurers down some of the most spectacular rivers in the country. Our commitment to safety, environmental conservation, and exceptional customer service has earned us numerous awards and certifications. Today, we operate with a fleet of over 30 rafts and employ more than 50 certified guides who share our passion for adventure and respect for the natural world.
